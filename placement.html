How does Cryptch Placement Work?

Cryptch assists students in getting job placements on successful completion of their courses. To make students job-ready, Cryptch conducts sessions on Resume building, how to do Group Discussions, how to behave in a Personal Interview, how to improve Communication Skills. From time to time, job fairs & campus recruitments are conducted. Also, students enhance their skills and knowledge by working on industry-based projects that feature real-time use cases. They obtain hands-on expertise in top IT skills, becoming industry-ready through our project works and assessments.
